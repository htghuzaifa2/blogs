The Internet of Things (IoT) has already transformed the way we interact with the world around us. Smart thermostats learn our preferences, and wearables track our fitness. But this is just the beginning. This post delves into the future of IoT, exploring the rise of smart cities, the integration of IoT with AI and 5G, and the potential for autonomous systems in transportation and industry. We'll also examine the security and privacy challenges that must be addressed as we move towards a more connected world.
