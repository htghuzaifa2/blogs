### 🌐 Check Online in 3 Clicks

**1. Universal Aggregator**
*   **Hamariweb Results** → [results.hamariweb.com](https://results.hamariweb.com/)
*   Enter roll number → select board → click “Check Result”.

**2. Board-Specific Portals**

| Board | Portal |

### 📄 Download & Print Options

*   **Provisional Marksheet**: PDF download available from the board portal immediately after the result is announced.
*   **Official Gazette**: A detailed PDF is uploaded 2 hours later on the board's site.
*   **Original Result Card**: Collect from your school after 3 working days.

### 🛠️ Troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Website overload** | Use the SMS method or an aggregator like Hamariweb. |
| **Wrong roll number** | Re-check your admit card; searching by name is not available. |
| **No SMS reply** | Re-send after 2 minutes; ensure there are no spaces in the roll number. |

### 🎯 One-Screen Cheat-Sheet

1.  Keep your roll number.
2.  On result day:
    *   **Online**: Go to board-portal.com → enter roll number.
    *   **SMS**: Send Roll Number → SMS code.
3.  Download PDF → share screenshot → celebrate.
