### 3. Fresh / Renewal ” Step-by-Step Online Process

#### A. Create Account

Go to **https://onlinemrp.dgip.gov.pk**
Click "Register" â†’ enter CNIC, mobile, email
Verify via OTP

#### B. Fill Application

Select "New" or "Renew"
Upload photo (auto-cropped tool provided)
Scan & upload CNIC front/back, old passport (if renewing)

#### C. Pay Fee

Choose Normal / Urgent / Fast-Track
Save payment receipt PDF (needed for appointment)

#### D. Book Appointment

Green slots = biometric + photograph
Blue slots = photograph only (kids < 12)
Print appointment slip with token number
