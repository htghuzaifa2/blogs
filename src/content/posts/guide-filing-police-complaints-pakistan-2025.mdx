### Before You File: Critical Prep Work

Gather Evidence:
- **Must-haves**: Photos/videos, CNIC, written incident description, witness contacts.
- **Digital Proof**: Timestamped recordings (audio/video) for bribery or threats.

Know Complaint Types:
- Non-FIR registration, illegal detention, false cases, bribery, negligence.

Avoid Retaliation:
- Never visit police stations alone; use encrypted apps (Signal/Telegram) to share evidence.
