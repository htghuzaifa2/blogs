Step-by-step paths for NAB (National Accountability Bureau) and FIA (Federal Investigation Agency) — choose the route that fits your case.

### 🎯 Decide First: NAB or FIA?

| Type of Corruption                                     | Where to Report |
| ------------------------------------------------------ | --------------- |
| Public-sector bribery, embezzlement, misuse of authority | NAB             |
| Bank fraud, cyber-crime, fake documents, private-sector fraud | FIA             |

### 📋 Reporting to NAB (National Accountability Bureau)

#### 1. Online Complaint (fastest)

**Portal**: www.nab.gov.pk → Public Complaints

**Steps**:

1.  Click “Lodge Complaint” → fill CNIC, details, evidence (PDF/JPG ≤ 2 MB)
2.  Receive tracking ID immediately via SMS & email.

#### 2. Email (for overseas Pakistanis)

**Email**: chairman@nab.gov.pk

**Format**:

```
Subject: Corruption Complaint – [City/Dept]
Body: Name, CNIC, phone, facts, evidence links/attachments
```

#### 3. In-Person / Postal

**Address**: Chairman NAB, NAB Headquarters, Islamabad
**Regional Offices**: [List & contacts](https://www.nab.gov.pk/contact-us.html)
**Open Hearings**: Last Thursday every month at Regional DGs—walk-in complaints accepted.

### 📋 Reporting to FIA (Federal Investigation Agency)

#### 1. Cyber-Enabled Financial Fraud / AML

**Portal**: complaint.fia.gov.pk

**Steps**:

1.  Select “Financial Fraud” → upload bank slips, chat logs, screenshots
2.  Tracking ID via SMS in 30 seconds.

#### 2. Email / WhatsApp / Call

| Channel    | Details                      |
| ---------- | ---------------------------- |
| Email      | helpdesk@nr3c.gov.pk         |
| WhatsApp   | 021-9901-3130 (save contact) |
| Helpline   | 1991 (Cyber Crime Wing, Mon–Fri 8 AM–4 PM) |

#### 3. In-Person

**Cyber Crime Reporting Centers**: [List & phone numbers](https://www.nr3c.gov.pk/contact.html)
**Required**: CNIC, evidence on USB/CD, written statement.

### 📄 What Evidence to Attach

| NAB                                            | FIA                                     |
| ---------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| - Copies of corrupt contracts, receipts        | - Bank statements, fake invoices        |
| - Audio/video recordings                       | - SMS/WhatsApp screenshots              |
| - Witness CNICs                                | - IP addresses, emails, URLs            |

### ⏱️ Timelines & Escalation

| Body | Acknowledge | Resolution  | Appeal          |
| ---- | ----------- | ----------- | --------------- |
| NAB  | 7 days      | 30–90 days  | Chairman Review |
| FIA  | 2 days      | 15–30 days  | DG FIA          |

### 🛠️ One-Screen Cheat-Sheet

1.  **Identify**: Public-sector corruption → NAB
2.  **Private / cyber fraud** → FIA
3.  **Upload evidence** → Portal / Email
4.  **Save tracking ID** → follow SMS updates
5.  **Escalate after 30 days** if no response

**Tip**: Always save screenshots and tracking IDs—they’re your golden ticket if you need to escalate further.
