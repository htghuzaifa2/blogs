*(Updated 18 August 2025 | Covers Punjab, Sindh, KPK, and Balochistan)*

### Eligibility Criteria

**Age Requirement:**

- 18+ years: For motorcycles and cars [9].
- 21+ years: For Light Transport Vehicles (LTV) [9].
- **Document**: Valid CNIC (or B-Form for minors).
- **Medical Certificate**: Mandatory for applicants over 50 [9].

### Required Documents

| Document            | Format                      | Notes                       |
|---------------------|-----------------------------|-----------------------------|
| CNIC Copy           | Front + Back scan           | Must be unexpired           |
| Passport-Sized Photos | 35x45mm, white background | 2 copies (digital upload)   |
| Medical Certificate | PDF (if applicable)         | Only for age 50+            |
| Fee Payment Proof   | Digital receipt             | Paid via JazzCash/EasyPaisa/bank |

### Step-by-Step Online Application

**1. Access Your Provincial Portal**

| Province     | Portal URL                 | Key Features                      |
|--------------|----------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| Punjab       | dlims.punjab.gov.pk        | Real-time tracking, SMS alerts    |
| Sindh        | dls.sindhpolice.gov.pk     | Integrated police verification    |
| KPK          | kpexcise.gov.pk            | Online appointment scheduling [15] |
| Balochistan  | In-person only             | Visit regional licensing offices  |

**2. Create Account & Apply**

1.  **Register**: CNIC + mobile number → verify via OTP [9].
2.  **Select Service**: Choose "Learner’s Permit" (not "Permanent License").
3.  **Upload Documents**: CNIC scan, photo, medical certificate (if required).

**3. Pay Fee**

- **Cost**: ₨ 500–₨ 2,000 (varies by province/vehicle type) [9].
- **Methods**: JazzCash, EasyPaisa, bank transfer, or credit card.

**4. Book Biometric Appointment**

- **Purpose**: Fingerprint/photo capture at licensing center.
- **Pro Tip**: "Book slots at 8 AM or 3 PM for shortest queues" [9].

**5. Download Permit**

- **Timeline**: Approved within 48 hours (Punjab/Sindh).
- **Validity**: 6 months (non-renewable) → upgrade to permanent license [9][17].

### Critical 2025 Updates

- **Punjab**: Permits now issued at 737 police stations (bypass DLIMS for urgent needs) [17].
- **KPK**: Use `ptpkp.gov.pk/onlineappointment` to book slots at Gulbahar HQ [15].
- **Fraud Alert**: Only use `.gov.pk` portals—avoid third-party agents.

### Post-Application Checklist

- **Carry Permit**: Always keep a digital/physical copy while driving.
- **Supervision Required**: Drive only with a licensed adult.
- **Permanent License Test**: Schedule within 4 months (tests fill fast!).
- **Track Status**: SMS application ID to `9966` (Punjab) or check portal dashboard.

### Troubleshooting

| Issue                     | Solution                                        |
|---------------------------|-------------------------------------------------|
| "Document rejected"       | Re-scan in 300 DPI; ensure CNIC is unexpired    |
| "Payment not reflected"   | Email receipt + CNIC to `support@dlims.gov.pk` |
| "No appointment slots"    | Check portals at 12 AM (new slots daily)        |
| "Biometric failure"       | Visit center with original CNIC + payment slip  |

### Pro Tips

- **Sindh Applicants**: Use the "Fast Track" option (₨ 1,000 extra) for 24-hour processing.
- **Students**: Discounted fee of ₨ 300 for motorcycle permits (show student ID).
- **Practice Tests**: Download DLIMS Prep app for road-sign quizzes.

### One-Screen Cheat Sheet

1.  **Prepare**: CNIC scan + photo + fee (₨500–₨2,000)
2.  **Portal**:
    -   Punjab/Sindh/KPK: Use provincial DLIMS link
    -   Balochistan: Visit office
3.  **Apply**: Select "Learner’s Permit" → Upload docs → Pay online
4.  **Biometrics**: Book slot → Visit center
5.  **Download**: Print permit → Drive with supervision!
