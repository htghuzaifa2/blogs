### Document Comparison & Legal Significance

| Certificate | Key Uses                                           | Issuing Authority                | Validity |
|-------------|----------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------|----------|
| Birth       | School admission, CNIC, passport, inheritance      | Union Council + NADRA            | Lifetime |
| Marriage    | Visa applications, spouse benefits, inheritance    | Union Council (Nikah Nama) + NADRA | Lifetime |
| Death       | Inheritance, bank account closure, insurance claims | Hospital + Union Council + NADRA | Lifetime |

### 1. Birth Certificate Application

**Requirements**

-   **Child**: B-Form/CRC (if available), hospital birth slip
-   **Parents**: CNICs, marriage certificate, Family Registration Certificate (FRC) [6][11]
-   **Biometrics**:
    -   Under 3 years: No biometrics
    -   3“10 years: Photo + iris scan
    -   Over 10 years: Full fingerprints [11]

**Online Process**

1.  **Download Form**: Get provincial birth form (e.g., Punjab Form) [7].
2.  **Upload via Pak ID App**: Scan documents â†’ Submit for Union Council verification [6][10].
3.  **Pay Fee**: Rs 200 (Pakistan), Rs 1,000 (overseas), Rs 2,000 (foreigners) [10].
4.  **Collect**: From Union Council or via courier (7“10 days).

**Critical Note**: NADRA does not issue birth certificates online”only verifies them post-registration [4].

### 2. Marriage Certificate (Nikah Nama) Registration

**Requirements**

-   Bride & groom CNICs
-   Witnesses' CNICs (2+ people)
-   Union Council marriage form [7]
-   Attested Nikah Nama from mosque/marriage hall

**Online Process**

1.  **Provincial Portal**: e.g., Punjab Marriage Registration â†’ Upload Nikah Nama + CNICs [7].
2.  **NADRA Update**: Log in to Pak Identity â†’ Add spouse details under "Family Tree" [3].
3.  **Pay Fee**: Rs 500“Rs 2,000 (varies by province).
4.  **Collect**: Digitally signed Nikah Nama from Union Council.

**Deadline**: Register within 60 days of marriage to avoid Rs 100/day penalty [7].

### 3. Death Certificate Application

**Requirements**

-   Hospital death report
-   Deceased™s CNIC copy
-   Applicant™s CNIC + proof of relationship
-   Affidavit (if hospital report unavailable) [2][12]

**Online Process**

1.  **Portal Access**:
    -   NADRA: Vital Registration Portal
    -   Provincial: e.g., Punjab Death Certificate [2].
2.  **Upload Documents**: Scan death report + CNICs.
3.  **Pay Fee**: Rs 50“Rs 1,000 (based on urgency) [2].
4.  **Track**: SMS `<application_id>` to `9966`.

### Critical Warnings

-   **Birth Certificate Fraud**: Verify Union Council stamps via NADRA™s SMS service [11].
-   **Unregistered Marriages**: Non-registration invalidates inheritance claims [7].
-   **Death Certificate Scams**: Only use `.gov.pk` portals”avoid agents charging "speed money" [2].

### Pro Tips for 2025

-   **Overseas Applicants**: Use Pak Identity for all certificates”consulates only assist with biometrics [3][5].
-   **Missing Documents**: For deaths outside hospitals, submit affidavits signed by a magistrate [12].
-   **Track Applications**:
    -   SMS `<CNIC>` to `7000` for birth/marriage certificates.
    -   Use NADRA™s Portal for real-time updates [1].
