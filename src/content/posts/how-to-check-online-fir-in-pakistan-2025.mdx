“Your FIR is now just one CNIC away—no police station visits required.”

Below are step-by-step links and apps to view, download, or verify a First Information Report (FIR) for Punjab, Sindh, Islamabad (ICT), and beyond.

### 🟢 Punjab Police – PRS Portal & Smart Police App

Punjab Police provides robust online services for all its districts, including Lahore, Rawalpindi, Multan, and Faisalabad.

| Tool | Steps | Direct Link / Download |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **PRS Web Portal** | 1. Go to `prs.punjabpolice.gov.pk` <br> 2. Select FIR type (e.g., Crime, Theft) <br> 3. Enter CNIC or FIR number + District | Web only |
| **Smart Police App** | 1. Install “Punjab Police Pakistan” (Android/iOS) <br> 2. Go to “Online Services” → “FIR Check” <br> 3. Enter details | Play Store / App Store |

### 🔵 Sindh Police – PRVS Portal

This portal covers Karachi, Hyderabad, Sukkur, Larkana, and all other districts in Sindh.

| Tool | Steps | Direct Link |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **PRVS Portal** | 1. Visit `prvs.sindhpolice.gov.pk` <br> 2. Enter CNIC or FIR Number <br> 3. Click "Search" | [prvs.sindhpolice.gov.pk](https://prvs.sindhpolice.gov.pk/) |

### 🟠 Islamabad (ICT) Police

The Islamabad Capital Territory police offer similar digital services.

| Tool | Steps | Direct Link / App |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **ICT Complaints Portal** | 1. Go to `complaints.islamabadpolice.gov.pk` <br> 2. Navigate to FIR section to search | [complaints.islamabadpolice.gov.pk](https://complaints.islamabadpolice.gov.pk/) |
| **ICT Police App** | Same flow as Punjab’s app (shared code base). | Play Store |

### 📄 Universal One-Screen Checklist

1.  Keep your **CNIC** or **FIR number** ready.
2.  Choose your province:
    *   **Punjab / ICT** → **Smart Police app** or **PRS web portal**.
    *   **Sindh** → **PRVS web portal**.
3.  Enter details → **Download PDF** → share or print.
