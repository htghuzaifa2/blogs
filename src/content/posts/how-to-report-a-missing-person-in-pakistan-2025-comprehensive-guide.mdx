## 🚨 Immediate Actions (First 24 Hours)

1.  **Gather Critical Info**:
    -   Recent photo, CNIC copy, last seen location/time
    -   Clothing description, medical conditions, phone IMEI (dial \*#06# from their phone)
2.  **Alert Close Network**:
    -   Contact friends, workplaces, hospitals
    -   Check location via Google Timeline (if shared) or Find My Device
3.  **Preserve Digital Evidence**:
    -   Save call logs/texts
    -   Secure social media passwords (for forensic access)

## 👮 Step 1: File FIR at Police Station

### 📋 Documents Required

| Document               | Notes                               |
| ---------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| CNIC of missing person | Copy (if available)                 |
| Recent photograph      | Clear face shot                     |
| Affidavit (Rs. 50 stamp) | Detailing circumstances             |
| Witness contact info   | Last seen with/phone calls          |

### 📍 Process

1.  Visit nearest police station (jurisdiction where person went missing).
2.  Demand FIR under **Section 346 PPC** (wrongful confinement) → Refusal is illegal.
3.  **Get FIR copy**: Required for all next steps.

**⚠️ If police refuse**:
-   Contact DSP Complaint Cell (Punjab: 1787; Sindh: 1715)
-   File online via [Punjab Police Portal](https://www.punjabpolice.gov.pk/) → "Missing Person" category

## 🌐 Step 2: Activate Digital Alerts

### 1. Punjab Police "Missing Person" Portal
-   Register at [Punjab Police Portal](https://www.punjabpolice.gov.pk/) → "Missing Persons"
-   Upload: FIR copy, photo, physical descriptors
-   Auto-alert: Broadcast to all police stations + highway patrols

### 2. Pakistan Citizen Portal (PCP)
-   Submit complaint → Tag "IGP [Province]" + "Ministry of Interior"
-   Attach FIR → Forces multi-agency coordination

### 3. NADRA's Biometric Tracking
-   Email FIR + photo to `missing@nadra.gov.pk` → Flags CNIC usage nationwide

-   **Roshni**: Issues SMS alerts to 5M+ subscribers

## 📱 Step 4: Leverage Public Outreach

-   **Social Media**:
    -   Post with hashtags: `#Find[Name]` + `#MissingInPakistan`
    -   Tag influencers: `@dutyofpakistan` (1.2M followers), `@pakistanupdates`
-   **Print Posters**:
    -   Use templates from `PakistanMissingPersons.org`
    -   Distribute at mosques, markets, transit hubs
-   **TV/Radio Alerts**:
    -   Contact Geo News (022-111-111-444) or FM 101 for public service announcements

| Punjab Police Portal           | Online search by CNIC/name                  | Punjab only  |

> 🔍 **Search Tip**: Check Punjab's Portal daily—updated hourly with found/unidentified persons.

## 💡 Prevention Measures

-   **NADRA Child Registration**: Link children to parents' CNIC via B-Form.
-   **GPS Trackers**: Use low-cost devices (₨1,500/month from Trakker.pk).
-   **Digital Footprint Kit**:
    -   Save fingerprints (inkless paper)
    -   Store DNA sample (hairbrush/toothbrush in sealed bag)
