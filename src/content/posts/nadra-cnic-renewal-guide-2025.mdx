### 🔐 Universal 3-Step Flow (Works Everywhere)

#### 1️⃣ Pre-Check
- Expiry date printed on the front of your CNIC
- Fingerprint issue? → see Section 7 below first

#### 2️⃣ Apply Online

| Platform             | URL                        | Use Case                          |
| -------------------- | -------------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Pak-Identity Portal  | pak-identity.nadra.gov.pk  | All renewals & modifications      |
| Pak ID Mobile App    | Play Store / App Store     | Photo + biometric upload on phone |

**Upload Checklist**
- CNIC front & back (JPG ≤ 500 KB)
- Passport-size photo (white background, 600 × 600 px)
- Thumbprint scans (PNG, ≥ 300 dpi)

#### 3️⃣ Pay & Track
- **Online**: HBL Konnect, JazzCash, credit/debit card
- **Track**: Enter tracking ID → tracking.nadra.gov.pk
- **SMS**: Send tracking ID to 9988 → status in 10 seconds

### 📱 Mobile App Shortcut (30-Second Walkthrough)

1. Open Pak ID App → “Renew CNIC”
2. Scan CNIC → auto-fills data
3. Capture photo → AI crop
4. Pay inside app → ₨ 750 for normal
5. Book courier → ₨ 250 home-delivery

### 🔧 Troubleshooting Cheat-Sheet

| Problem                 | Quick Fix                                     |
| ----------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| Fingerprint mismatch    | Visit NADRA e-Sahulat → biometric update (free)|
| Photo rejected          | Retake inside Pak-Identity portal or mobile app|
| Status stuck            | Wait 3–7 working days for NADRA sync          |
| 938 code error          | Re-sync CNIC + BISP at BISP Tehsil Office     |

### 🚨 Fingerprint Mismatch → Step-by-Step Fix

1. Clean fingers (soap + warm water)
2. Visit nearest NADRA office with original CNIC
3. Request “Biometric Update” (free)
4. Re-scan all 10 fingers
5. Wait 3–7 days → retry BISP or passport applications
