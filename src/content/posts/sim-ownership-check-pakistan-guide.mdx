*(Updated 18 August 2025 | Official PTA Methods for SIM Verification, Blocking & Device Security)*

### Part 1: Check SIMs Registered Under Your CNIC

**Method 1: SMS to 668 (Instant)**

1.  **Compose SMS**: Type your 13-digit CNIC (without dashes, e.g., 4220112345678).
2.  **Send to 668**: Cost: Rs 2 + tax [3][5].
3.  **Receive Details**: Reply lists all active SIMs, operators, and registration dates.

**Method 2: PTA Website (Detailed Report)**

1.  Visit `cnic.sims.pk` [7][11].
2.  Enter CNIC + solve CAPTCHA.
3.  View table showing:
    -   Mobile numbers linked to your CNIC
    -   Network operators (Jazz/Zong/Ufone/Telenor)
    -   Biometric verification status
    -   Registration status (active/inactive) [11].

**Method 3: MyPTA App**

1.  Download MyPTA (Google Play/App Store).
2.  Register with CNIC + mobile number.
3.  Access real-time SIM details [3].

### Part 2: Block Unauthorized or Lost SIMs

**Step 1: Block via Mobile Operator**

1.  **Visit Franchise**: Carry original CNIC + copies to operator™s outlet (Jazz/Telenor/Zong/Ufone) [11].
2.  **Submit Form**: Request immediate blocking of unauthorized SIMs.
3.  **Helpline Support**:

| Operator | Helpline     |
|----------|--------------|
| Jazz     | 111/321      |
| Zong     | 310          |
| Ufone    | 333          |
| Telenor  | 345          |

**Step 2: Report to PTA (If Operator Fails)**

-   **Email**: Send evidence to `help@pta.gov.pk` [7][11].

### Critical 2025 Deadlines

-   **Deceased Users**: SIMs under expired CNICs blocked after 15 Sept 2025 (submit death certificate to transfer ownership) [12][15].
-   **Expired CNICs**: Renew by 2 Sept 2025 to avoid blocking [15].

### Part 3: Block Stolen/Lost Phones via DIRBS

**Method 1: PTA DIRBS Portal**

1.  Visit `dirbs.pta.gov.pk` â†’ "Block Lost/Stolen Device".
2.  Enter device IMEI (dial *#06# to find IMEI) [6][11].

**Method 2: PTA CMS App**

1.  Install PTA CMS (Device Blocking App).
2.  Log in â†’ Enter IMEI + police report copy.
3.  Track blocking status via SMS alerts [6].

**Note**: Blocked phones cannot be used on any Pakistani network.

### Critical 2025 Security Updates

-   **Biometric Verification Mandatory**: All SIMs require NADRA biometric verification [11][15].
-   **SIM Limit**: Max 5 SIMs/CNIC [11].
-   **Deceased User Cleanup**: 163,200+ illegal SIMs blocked since June 2025 [12].
-   **Third-Party App Warning**: Avoid unofficial "SIM owner detail" apps”PTA does not endorse them [9][13].

### Troubleshooting & Pro Tips

| Issue                           | Solution                                        |
|---------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------|
| "SMS to 668 failed"             | Ensure no dashes in CNIC; retry after 30 mins [5]. |
| "cnic.sims.pk not loading"      | Use Chrome/Firefox; disable VPN [11].           |
| "IMEI blocking rejected"        | Submit police FIR copy via PTA CMS app [6].     |
| "SIM still active after blocking" | Escalate to PTA via helpline 0800-55055 [7].   |

**Prevent Fraud:**

-   Verify unknown numbers by texting `MNP` to `667` (free) [13].
-   Check SIMs monthly via `668` to detect unauthorized registrations early [5].
