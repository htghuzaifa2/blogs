### Critical Precautions

**Verify Before Paying:**

- Match violation photo evidence (available on PSCA/Karachi portals) [4][11].
- Check for duplicate challans.

**Avoid Scams:**

- Never pay fines to personal mobile numbers.
- Official portals never ask for OTPs beyond login [3].

**Prevent Future Fines:**

- Enable SMS alerts on PSCA portal for real-time violations [4].
- Use Waze or Google Maps for speed camera alerts in Punjab.
