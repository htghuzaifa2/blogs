### Complaint Channels by Service

#### 1. Punjab Metrobus (Lahore/Islamabad-Rawalpindi)

**Online:**

- **Facebook:** Message [Punjab Masstransit Authority](https://www.facebook.com/punjabmasstransit) with details (e.g., station, bus number, issue).
- **PMA Portal:** Use the **Punjab Citizen Portal** app → Select "Transport" category.

**In-Person:**

- Visit station managers at major hubs (e.g., Qaddafi Station, Lahore; Secretariat Station, Islamabad).

**Key Issues Addressed:**

- Metro card shortages (report via Facebook).
- Escalator/AC failures (e.g., Kamahan Station).

#### 2. Karachi Green Line & BRT

**TransKarachi GRM Portal:**

- Visit [transkarachi.pk/grm](https://transkarachi.pk/grm) → Fill grievance form.
- Upload evidence (photos/videos; max 30 MB).

**Contact:**

- **Email:** complaint.grm@transkarachi.pk
- **Phone:** 021-111 544 278.

**LOT-Specific Offices:**

- **LOT-1 (Airport-Mosmiyat):** Opposite CP-6 Masjid, Aisha Road.
- **LOT-2 (Mosmiyat-Numaish):** Bagh Jinnah, Old Aladin Park.

#### 3. Daewoo Express Nationwide

**Terminal Complaints:**

- Visit any terminal (e.g., Lahore: Tokhar Niaz Baig; Karachi: Suhrab Goth) → Lodge with station manager.

**Helplines:**

- **Lahore:** 042-37498515
- **Islamabad:** 051-5466215
- **Karachi:** 021-6830082.

**Head Office:**

- 231-A Ferozepur Road, Lahore → Escalate unresolved issues here.

#### 4. Provincial Bus Services (e.g., Faisalabad, Peshawar)

- **Peshawar BRT:** Use KPPOLICE Online Portal → Select "Transport".
- **Sindh Peoples Bus:** WhatsApp 0311-011-1107 with bus number + route details.

### Critical 2025 Updates & Pro Tips

- **Punjab Metrobus Solarization:** Report station power failures via Facebook → Prioritized fixes during solar upgrades.
- **Karachi’s 24-Hr Rule:** Green Line complaints must include timestamped evidence for swift action.
- **Daewoo’s Silent Issue:** Overcrowding? SMS bus number + route to 3434 (nationwide).
- **Avoid Delays:** For broken ACs/escalators, tag @PunjabMasstransit on Twitter + PMA Facebook.

### Complaint Template for Maximum Impact

```plaintext
Subject: [Service] Complaint - [Issue] at [Location]
Body:
- Bus/Terminal: __________
- Time/Date: __________
- Issue: (e.g., "No metro cards at Islamabad station since 10 Aug")
- Evidence: Attach photo/video
- Demand: (e.g., "Restock cards by 25 Aug")
```
**Example:** *"Green Line Complaint - Broken ticketing machine at Numaish Station on 19 Aug, 10:30 AM. Demand: Repair in 48 hrs."*

### When All Else Fails: Nuclear Options

- **Pakistan Citizen Portal (PCP):** File complaint → Tag "Chief Secretary Punjab/Sindh".
- **Ombudsman:** For theft/harassment: Submit at Provincial Mohtasib office with PCP token.
- **Media Leverage:** Tweet #PunjabMetroFail or #GreenLineChaos with evidence → Forces public response.
