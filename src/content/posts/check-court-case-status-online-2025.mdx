“Courts went digital in 2025; your case file is now one CNIC or case-number away.”

Below are the official, up-to-date portals for District, High, and Supreme Courts—plus mobile apps and SMS alerts. Bookmark this page before your next hearing.

### 1. Supreme Court of Pakistan (SCP)

| Portal | What You Get | Quick Link |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Case Status** | Bench, next hearing date, orders | [supremecourt.gov.pk/online-case-status](https://supremecourt.gov.pk/online-case-status) |
| **Cause List** | Daily / weekly hearing schedules | [supremecourt.gov.pk/cause-list](https://supremecourt.gov.pk/cause-list) |
| **Judgement Search** | Full PDFs of verdicts & orders | [supremecourt.gov.pk/judgements](https://supremecourt.gov.pk/judgements) |

*Requires Case Type + Number + Year (at least 2 fields).*

### 2. High Courts (All Provinces)

| High Court | Portal / App | Link |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lahore High Court (LHC)** | Case Management + Mobile App | [lhc.gov.pk/case_management](https://lhc.gov.pk/case_management) & LHC App |
| **Sindh High Court (SHC)** | CFMS Portal | [cases.shc.gov.pk](https://cases.shc.gov.pk/) |
| **Islamabad High Court (IHC)** | IHC MIS Portal | [mis.ihc.gov.pk/frmCseSrch](https://mis.ihc.gov.pk/frmCseSrch) |
| **Peshawar High Court** | Online Case Search | [peshawarhighcourt.gov.pk](http://peshawarhighcourt.gov.pk/app/site/15/p/Search_For_Case.html) |
| **Balochistan High Court (BHC)** | BHC Portal | [portal.bhc.gov.pk/case-status](https://portal.bhc.gov.pk/case-status) |

**Required Inputs (any one):**
*   Case number + year
*   Party name
*   CNIC / Advocate enrollment number

### 3. District Courts (Punjab, Sindh, KP, AJK)

| Province | System | Portal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Punjab** (All 41 districts) | CFMS Punjab | [dsj.punjab.gov.pk](https://dsj.punjab.gov.pk/) |
| **Sindh** (All 33 districts) | CFMS Sindh | [cases.districtcourtssindh.gos.pk](http://cases.districtcourtssindh.gos.pk/) |
| **KP** (Peshawar & others) | CFMS KP | [cfms-kp.gov.pk](https://cfms-kp.gov.pk/) |
| **AJK** | AJK District CFMS | [ajkdistrictcourts.gok.pk/case-search](https://ajkdistrictcourts.gok.pk/case-search) |

*Both case number + year or CNIC work.*

### 4. Mobile Apps (Real-Time Alerts)

| App | Courts Covered | Download |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **LHC Case Management** | Lahore High Court + Punjab Districts | Play Store / App Store |
| **Smart Courts (previously ICTP)** | Punjab District & High Courts | Play Store / App Store |
| **SHC CFMS** | Sindh High Court + Districts | Play Store / App Store |

### 5. One-Screen Cheat-Sheet

1.  **Identify your court level:**
    *   Supreme Court → `supremecourt.gov.pk`
    *   High Court → Individual portal (see table)
    *   District → CFMS portal (see table)
2.  Enter **Case No. + Year** or **CNIC**.
3.  Download PDF / check hearing schedule.
4.  Set **SMS alert** (if the portal offers it).

Bookmark this page—your next hearing is literally one click away.
