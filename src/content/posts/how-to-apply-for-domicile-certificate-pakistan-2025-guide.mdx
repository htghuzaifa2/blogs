### Step-by-Step Application Process

#### 1. Punjab (e-Khidmat Centers)
1.  **Pay Challan:** Rs. 200 at National Bank.
2.  **Fill Forms:** Download Form P + P1 from e-Khidmat Portal.
3.  **Submit:** Visit e-Khidmat Markaz (e.g., Lahore Town Hall) with documents.
4.  **Track:** SMS application ID to 9100 or use Punjab Domicile Management System portal.
5.  **Collect:** In 16 days.

#### 2. Sindh (DC Office)
1.  **Pay Challan:** Rs. 200"“300 at designated bank.
2.  **Attach Documents:** Include Police Verification Report for Karachi/Larkana.
3.  **Submit:** At district DC office (e.g., DC Central for Nazimabad residents).
4.  **Wait:** 7"“14 days"”no online tracking.

#### 3. Islamabad (Citizen Facilitation Center)
1.  **Book Slot:** Walk into G-11/4 center (Mon"“Sat 9 AM"“8 PM).
2.  **Data Entry:** Staff uploads documents to system.
3.  **Pay Fee:** Rs. 300 at counter â†’ get e-receipt.
4.  **Collect:** Return in 7 days with receipt.

#### 4. KPK (Manual Process)
1.  Visit Tehsil Office with property papers/voter ID.
2.  Submit application â†’ wait for patwari verification.
3.  Processing time: 15"“30 days.

### Critical Warnings
- **One Domicile Only:** Illegal to hold multiple certificates"”cancel old one first.
- **Sindh Scams:** Verify DC office authenticity"”fake domiciles rampant in Karachi.
- **Address Mismatch:** If CNIC shows dual addresses, obtain NOC from "non-domicile" district.
- **Women Applicants:** Married women need husband"™s CNIC + marriage certificate.

### Pro Tips for 2025
- **Students:** Sindh/KPK universities no longer require student domiciles"”use father"™s certificate.
- **Overseas Pakistanis:** Apply via agencies like MamooInPakistan for document procurement.
