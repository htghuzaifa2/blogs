“Courts went digital in 2025; your case file is now one CNIC or case-number away.”

Below are the official, up-to-date portals for District, High, and Supreme Courts—plus mobile apps and SMS alerts. Bookmark this page before the next hearing.

### 1️⃣ Supreme Court of Pakistan (SCP)

| Portal             | What You Get                  | Quick Link                                     |
| ------------------ | ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Case Status        | Bench, next date, orders      | supremecourt.gov.pk/online-case-status         |
| Cause List         | Daily / weekly schedules      | supremecourt.gov.pk/cause-list                 |
| Judgement Search   | Full PDFs of verdicts         | supremecourt.gov.pk/judgements                 |

Requires Case Type + Number + Year (minimum 2 fields).

### 2️⃣ High Courts (All Provinces)

| High Court                  | Portal / App                      | Link                                                                 |
| --------------------------- | --------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Lahore High Court (LHC)     | Case Management + Mobile App      | lhc.gov.pk/case_management & LHC App                                 |
| Sindh High Court (SHC)      | CFMS Portal                       | cases.shc.gov.pk                                                     |
| Islamabad High Court (IHC)  | IHC MIS Portal                    | mis.ihc.gov.pk/frmCseSrch                                            |
| Peshawar High Court         | Online Case Search                | peshawarhighcourt.gov.pk/app/site/15/p/Search_For_Case.html          |
| Balochistan High Court (BHC)| BHC Portal                        | portal.bhc.gov.pk/case-status                                        |

Required Inputs (any one):
- Case number + year
- Party name
- CNIC / Advocate enrollment

### 3️⃣ District Courts (Punjab, Sindh, KP, AJK)

| Province                   | System            | Portal                               |
| -------------------------- | ----------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Punjab (All 41 districts)  | CFMS Punjab       | dsj.punjab.gov.pk                    |
| Sindh (All 33 districts)   | CFMS Sindh        | cases.districtcourtssindh.gos.pk     |
| KP (Peshawar & others)     | CFMS KP           | cfms-kp.gov.pk                       |
| AJK                        | AJK District CFMS | ajkdistrictcourts.gok.pk/case-search |

Both case number + year or CNIC work.

### 4️⃣ Mobile Apps (Real-Time Alerts)

| App                  | Courts Covered                    | Download     |
| -------------------- | --------------------------------- | ------------ |
| LHC Case Management  | Lahore High Court + Punjab Districts | Play Store   |
| ICTP (Smart Courts)  | Punjab District & High Courts     | Play Store   |
| SHC CFMS             | Sindh High Court + Districts      | Play Store   |

### 5️⃣ One-Screen Cheat-Sheet

```
1. Identify your court level:
   • Supreme Court → supremecourt.gov.pk/online-case-status
   • High Court → individual portal (see table)
   • District → CFMS portal (see table)
2. Enter **Case No + Year** or **CNIC**
3. Download PDF / schedule hearing
4. Set **SMS alert** (if portal offers)
```

Bookmark this page—your next hearing is literally one click away.
