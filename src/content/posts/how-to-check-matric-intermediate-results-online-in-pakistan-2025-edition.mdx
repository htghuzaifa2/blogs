### 🌐 Check Online in 3 Clicks

#### 1️⃣ Universal Aggregator

Hamariweb Results → results.hamariweb.com
Enter roll number → select board → click “Check Result”.

#### 2️⃣ Board-Specific Portals

| Board            | Portal                        |

### 📄 Download & Print Options

-   **Provisional Marksheet**: PDF download from board portal immediately after result.
-   **Official Gazette**: PDF uploaded 2 hours later on board site.
-   **Original Result Card**: Collect from school after 3 working days.

### 🛠️ Troubleshooting

| Problem               | Fix                                             |
| --------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Website overload      | Use SMS or Hamariweb aggregator.                |
| Wrong roll number     | Re-check admit card; name search is not available. |
| No SMS reply          | Re-send after 2 minutes; ensure no spaces in roll.|

### 🎯 One-Screen Cheat-Sheet

1.  Keep your 14-digit roll number.
2.  On result day:
    -   Online: board-portal.com → enter roll
    -   SMS: Roll → code
3.  Download PDF → share screenshot → celebrate.
