### 2025 Eligibility Rules

| Province   | Residency Requirement        | Special Criteria                                |
|------------|------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------|
| Punjab     | 1+ year in district          | Born in Punjab OR parents hold Punjab domicile [16] |
| Sindh      | 10+ years OR born in Sindh   | Father’s domicile accepted for students [10][15]  |
| KPK        | 1+ year                      | Property ownership OR voter registration [16]   |
| Islamabad  | 1+ year                      | Must cancel previous domicile first [3][16]     |

**Critical Change**: Sindh now allows college admissions on father’s domicile—no student certificate needed [10].

### Step-by-Step Application Process

**1. Punjab (e-Khidmat Centers)**

1.  **Pay Challan**: Rs. 200 at National Bank [16].
2.  **Fill Forms**: Download Form P + P1 from e-Khidmat Portal.
3.  **Submit**: Visit e-Khidmat Markaz (e.g., Lahore Town Hall) with documents.
4.  **Track**: SMS application ID to 9100 or use Punjab Domicile Management System portal.
5.  **Collect**: In 16 days [16].

**2. Sindh (DC Office)**

1.  **Pay Challan**: Rs. 200–300 at designated bank [15].
2.  **Attach Documents**: Include Police Verification Report for Karachi/Larkana [15].
3.  **Submit**: At district DC office (e.g., DC Central for Nazimabad residents).
4.  **Wait**: 7–14 days—no online tracking [15][16].

**3. Islamabad (Citizen Facilitation Center)**

1.  **Book Slot**: Walk into G-11/4 center (Mon–Sat 9 AM–8 PM) [3].
2.  **Data Entry**: Staff uploads documents to system.
3.  **Pay Fee**: Rs. 300 at counter → get e-receipt.
4.  **Collect**: Return in 7 days with receipt [3][16].

**4. KPK (Manual Process)**

1.  Visit Tehsil Office with property papers/voter ID.
2.  Submit application → wait for patwari verification.
3.  Processing time: 15–30 days [16].

### Critical Warnings

-   **One Domicile Only**: Illegal to hold multiple certificates—cancel old one first [3][16].
-   **Sindh Scams**: Verify DC office authenticity—fake domiciles rampant in Karachi [13].
-   **Address Mismatch**: If CNIC shows dual addresses, obtain NOC from "non-domicile" district [3].
-   **Women Applicants**: Married women need husband’s CNIC + marriage certificate [15][16].

### Troubleshooting Table

| Issue                         | Solution                                      |
|-------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| "Father’s domicile unavailable" | Submit grandfather’s certificate + birth proof [15] |
| "Document rejected"           | Re-attest with tehsil magistrate (not notary) |
| "Delay in verification"       | Escalate to Assistant Commissioner (Saddar) [3] |
| "Deceased parent"             | Submit death certificate + guardian’s documents [10] |

### Pro Tips for 2025

-   **Students**: Sindh/KPK universities no longer require student domiciles—use father’s certificate [6][10].
-   **Overseas Pakistanis**: Apply via agencies like MamooInPakistan for document procurement [9].
-   **Track Online**: Punjab offers SMS tracking; other provinces require office visits.
-   **Avoid Touts**: Official fees ≤ Rs. 300—never pay "speed money" [15].

### One-Screen Cheat Sheet

1.  **Prepare**: CNIC, father’s domicile, utility bills, affidavit
2.  **Province Portal**:
    -   Punjab: ekhidmat.punjab.gov.pk
    -   Islamabad: Citizen Center G-11/4
3.  **Pay Fee**: Rs. 200–300 via bank challan
4.  **Submit**: In-person at DC/e-Khidmat office
5.  **Collect**: 7–30 days (save e-receipt!)
