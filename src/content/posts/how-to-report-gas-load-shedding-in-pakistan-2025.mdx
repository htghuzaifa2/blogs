“Remember, the tracking ID is your proof—don’t lose it.”

### 1. SNGPL (Northern: Punjab, KP, AJK)

#### 🔹 One-Number Helpline

| Channel      | Contact | Working Hours |
| :----------- | :------ | :------------ |
| 24×7 Helpline | 1199    | Always open   |

#### 🔹 Online Portal

**Website**: [www.sngpl.com.pk](http://www.sngpl.com.pk) → Lodge Complaint

**Steps**
1.  Online Services → Complaint Registration
2.  Fill Consumer No., Address, Complaint Type (Low Pressure / Load-shedding)
3.  Upload photo of meter (optional but speeds things up)
4.  Submit → receive tracking ID instantly

#### 🔹 SMS & Mobile App

*   **SMS**: Send “COMP <Consumer-No><Message> to 1199
*   **Android / iOS App**: “SNGPL ConnectOn” – register complaint, track, attach photos

#### 🔹 Regional Complaint Centers

| City | Phone | Address |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Lahore Central | 042-9908 2000 | PITC, 22-A Queen’s Road |
| Multan | 061-922 0079 | Piran Ghaib Road |
| Islamabad | 051-926 8881 | Blue Area |
*Full list: [SNGPL Offices](https://www.sngpl.com.pk/page/93)*

### 2. SSGC (Southern: Sindh & Balochistan)

#### 🔹 Helpline & WhatsApp

| Channel | Contact |
| :--- | :--- |
| 24×7 Helpline | 021-9901 3130 |
| WhatsApp | 021-9901 3130 (save as contact) |

#### 🔹 Online Portal

**Website**: [www.ssgc.com.pk](http://www.ssgc.com.pk) → Complaint/Feedback

**Timeline**
*   Low pressure / load-shedding: 24–36 hrs
*   Excessive billing: 15 working days

#### 🔹 SMS

*   **SMS**: “COMP <Consumer-No><Message> to 9901 3130

### 3. Escalation Ladder (If 15 Days Pass)

| Level | Who | Contact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1. DISCO GM | Your Regional GM (see tables) | Phone numbers above |
| 2. OGRA | [complaints.ogra.org.pk](https://complaints.ogra.org.pk) | 30-day resolution |
| 3. Prime Minister’s Portal | [citizenportal.gov.pk](https://citizenportal.gov.pk) | Auto-escalates after 21 days |

### 4. Documents You Need

*   Latest bill photo (front & back)
*   Meter photo showing actual reading
*   CNIC copy (if complaint via portal)

### 5. One-Screen Cheat-Sheet

1.  Take meter photo → Note actual reading
2.  SMS “COMP <consumer-no> Load-shedding” to 1199 (SNGPL) or 021-9901 3130 (SSGC)
3.  Upload to DISCO portal → Save tracking ID
4.  Track via app / website / SMS
5.  Escalate to OGRA after 15 days

**Pro-tip**: Share the tracking ID on Twitter @SNGPL1199 or @SSGCOfficial—public tweets get faster attention.
