## How to Download Date Sheet

### Step 1: Official Link
*   **English site:** https://pseb.ac.in
*   **Urdu site:** https://pseb.ac.in/urdu

### Step 2: Click & Save
Homepage → “Date Sheet 2025” → Download PDF → Save to phone gallery → WhatsApp to study group.

### Step 3: Backup Plan
If site crashes (it will), use SMS:
> Type **PB10** <space>
> Send to **5676750**
You’ll get date-sheet + result alerts on your phone.

## Real-Life Tips (from students who survived the drum-roll)

*   **Photocopy the date-sheet** — stick on wall, stick on fridge, stick on mom’s forehead — visual memory > panic memory.
*   **Mock papers at 11 a.m.** — train your brain to be alert at exam time, not at midnight.
*   **Practical prep early** — Jan 27 is closer than you think — practice titrations now, not on Jan 26.
*   **Backup stationery** — two pens, two pencils, two calculators — because Murphy’s Law loves exam halls.
*   **Mental health > mark sheet** — breathe, break, binge on biryani — then back to books.

## Post-Exam Roadmap (after the drum-roll stops)

*   **Result Date:** May 16, 10:00 a.m. — check via SMS, website, or “mummy’s WhatsApp group”.
*   **Passing Marks:** 33 % in each subject — 33 is the new 100 (until you cross it).
*   **Supplementary Exams:** Aug 8–29 — second chance, not second class — treat it like overtime, not overtime stress.
