### 2. Choose the Right Solar Panels

Pakistan mein ab polycrystalline aur monocrystalline panels common hain.

- **Monocrystalline panels** thode mehngay hain lekin zyada efficient.

Budget option ke liye 2 ya 3 150-200W panels start ke liye kaafi hain.
