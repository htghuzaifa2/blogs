Below is the 2025 up-to-date roadmap for checking Matric-to-PhD merit lists of Pakistan’s top universities without ever standing in a queue.

### 🚀 Universal 3-Step Check

1.  Keep your roll number or CNIC ready.
2.  Visit the university (or UET/NUST/FAST etc.) portal below.
3.  Enter credentials → download PDF → celebrate.
