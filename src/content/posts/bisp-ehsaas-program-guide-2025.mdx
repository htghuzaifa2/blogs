### üìù Step-by-Step Application

#### New NSER Registration (Mandatory First Step)
1.  **Locate Center**: Find nearest Ehsaas Registration Center via SMS ADDRESS to 8171.
2.  **Documents**: CNIC, children's B-Forms, utility bill, income proof.
3.  **Biometric Verification**: Fingerprint scan for all adults in household.
4.  **Survey**: Answer NSER questions on assets/income honestly ‚Äì determines PMT score.

#### Taleemi Wazaif (Education Stipend) Enrollment
Visit BISP Office with:
- Child's B-Form/CRC (NADRA-verified)
- School admission slip (stamped by institution)
- Beneficiary mother's CNIC
- **Attendance Compliance**: Maintain 70% school attendance quarterly to continue receiving stipends.
